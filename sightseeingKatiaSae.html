Sightseeing on the path of Katia Sae


New Eden Voyages is proud to present a new experience, a new voyage through the stars of New Eden.

Board the NEV Voyager I and let our hosts lead you through the most awe inspiring and exciting space tour of the whole cluster.

The Safe Thrill Experience

This voyage will carry you through the four Great Empires and will touch some of most famous monuments this side of the cluster. Everything will happen under the watchful eye of CONCORD and the Empires' police forces.

Notable sights: Katia Sae Monument, Battle of Iyen-Oursta Monument

The Maximum Thrill Experience

This voyage will carry you throughout the whole cluster, from the safest systems of High Security space, to the most lawless reaches of Null Security space. Feel on your skin the thrill of dipping into areas of spaces where there is no law but the law of guns. Hold your breath as we sneak through haunted systems and around hungry capsuleers.

Notable sights: Titanomachy, A History of War


The Extreme Thrill Experience

This Voyage is not for the faint of heart. Null Security space is not enough? With the Extreme Thrill experience we will add on the Maximum Thrill Experience by diving into the mysterious Anoikis. Prepare for a longer trip as we will hunt wormhole after wormhole for secret hideouts and fabled treasures.

Notable sights: Redbout, Thera
